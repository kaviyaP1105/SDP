VEGETABLE SALAD

Benefits of Vegetable Salad

Aside from their natural good taste and great cunchy texture alongside wonderful colors and fragrances,eating a large serving of fresh,raw vegetables each day can have significant health benefits.

Steps to make Vegetable Salad

  • Take a large and clear bowl.
  • Try adding blanched,lightly seamed or roasted veggies like broccoli,french beans,cauliflower,baby corn.
  • Add lentils,beans or chickenpeas or beab sprouts for more protins.
  • Grate some of the vegetables for different texture.
  • Add marinated veggies like arichoke hearts for tang,or other veggies like raddish for some pugency.
  • Swag in your own favouits combination of dried fruits,nuts and seeds.
  • Use fresh coriander or chives instead of parley
This easy vegetable salad is packed with a variety of summer produce with all the textures and colors one could ever want.
